OF THE BOX GYM


Omnisport is not a "one size fits all" kind of place. Everyone has different capabilities and goals, so we work with you to create a customized program that meets your individual needs.

MOVE WELL. MOVE OFTEN.

OmniSport coaches are here to empower, motivate, and educate you every step of the way. How we do that is rooted in our philosophy to "Move well. Move Often." We succeed in this by assessing every client's level of fitness through seven core movements called The Functional Movement Screen test. This test evaluates your functional movement quality which allows for a reliable baseline for actionable and effective steps to improve your performance and recovery.


Seven Movements

Use the screening to determine your current level of movement quality.

Custom Programs

Train and track your results to push to the next level and enjoy a better quality of life.

SERVICES


UNLOCK YOUR POTENTIAL.

This is a program that is designed for you. As a member, the program is a unique roadmap to your goals for your body type and level of fitness. What we know is that no one program works for everyone and no program will work forever. We think as you and your body change so should your program and our services reflect that.


Group Classes

Included for every member of Omnisport. Come work out with Coach Cari every Saturday morning for a heart rate workout using a variety of modalities and tools!

Semi Private Personal Training

For a discounted rate— train in groups of 2-6! Each client will receive an individual program and in person coaching from one of our staff.

Private Personal Training

A fully customizable experience with our elite trainers. You will receive a personalized program, one on one coaching, and a free heart rate monitor to help track your progress in session!

Recovery + Bodywork

Proper exercise requires proper recovery: add cupping, myofascial blading, athletic taping, dynamic air compression massage, and more to your training regimen.

Nutritional Guide to Follow

Work with one of our certified nutrition coaches to help you find balance outside of the gym. Book a session today to take a deep dive into your diet and explore how nutrition and exercise can work in tandem to better your health.

Virtual Training

Not local? That's okay — OmniSport also offers virtual training for both exercise programs and nutrition consultations. Contact us today to schedule your first session!
